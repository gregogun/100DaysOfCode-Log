by Greg Ogun

Greetings, friends! (Or as my first programmer-baby words were: "Hello World!" If you're reading this, chances are you're a fellow developer or maybe a hiring manager (please please please be a hiring manager) and you might be so curious to find out whether my taking on this challenge will result in my rising to glory or a painful, long-winded descent into madness. Well, perhaps you could stick around and find out.

Shall we get into it?

Disclaimer: You might notice that before Day 15 things might look a bit, well, boring. (Day 15 is when I had a small epiphany and decided to start keeping a better log of my 100 days.) So, if you want to get to the more wholesome content, do feel free to skip ahead, or watch me struggle along till my Day 15 epiphany.
