Day 15: 29th June, 2020

Today’s Progress

  • Got back into #100DaysOfCode after some much-needed days off.
  • Went back to JS basics and started reading Jon Duckett's 'JavaScript & jQuery' book.
  • Signed up to Codeacademy!

Thoughts: Wasn't easy making the decision to kinda go back to basics with JavaScript but I know it will be worth it's weight in gold in the long run (so I hope).

Day 16: 30th June, 2020

Today’s Progress:

  • Read through 'What is JavaScript' on MDN Docs. Learnt some useful stuff that other intro docs/tutorials didn't mention such as 'defer' and the difference between 'async' and 'defer'.
  • Started watching 90 seconds JavaScript series by codeSTACKr
  • Wrote some code (half-awake and all by myself!) in the editor, before bed.
.

Thoughts: I feel like I did okay today considering doing an 8 hour shift at work. I realised that it might be more effective to started coding not too long after I get home and leave other activities for the evening as the tiredness hits hard when coding late after a full days. I mean, can I really say I'm surprised? 🤦🏾‍♂️

Note: It's a new month woo! This is beginning of my second month learning coding and what a rollercoaster it's been already. I will say though, I'm pretty proud of the progress I've made thus far, and I hope to keep consistent and learning (and hitting those goals!) throughout July!💪🏾

Today’s Progress:
